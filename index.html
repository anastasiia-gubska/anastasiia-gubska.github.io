About Me

I'm Anastasiia, a SRE/DevOps Engineer at BT Group. As an active member of the CNCF Deaf and Hard of Hearing Working Group (DHHWG) and co-organizer of Deaf in Cloud Native meetups, I'm committed to fostering a supportive community for Deaf and Hard of Hearing professionals in the cloud native space.

Technical Pursuits

With a deep interest in technology, I chose a career path in SRE and DevOps, where I specialize in cloud infrastructure, automation, and problem solving. My work involves implementing reliable systems, optimizing performance, and ensuring that applications and services run smoothly.

Advocacy and Community Building

Joining the CNCF DHHWG has been a rewarding experience, allowing me to demonstrate my technical skills and advocate for accessibility and inclusion within the tech industry in parallel. Through this work, I aim to create more opportunities and support for Deaf and Hard of Hearing professionals in the cloud native ecosystem.
